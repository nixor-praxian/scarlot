03 · Concept Catalogue

7 Concepts,
Derived from Evidence

C-01 · Priority: Immediate
Shared Blacklist Network
A structured, searchable database of flagged client numbers — personal or collective. The data already exists in Telegram groups. It's just not organised or discoverable. Centralising it creates value from day one.
P3 P5 P6 P7 #safety #collective-intelligence
Tension: personal privacy vs. collective value. Users must choose their sharing level.
C-02 · Priority: Immediate
Conversational Interface
No new app. No new UI. All functionality delivered through natural language in WhatsApp or Telegram. A voice note cancels your day. A text checks a blacklist. The agent assists — it never impersonates.
P1 P4 P8 #agent #no-interface
Constraint: community resistance to AI replacing human interaction. Semi-auto is required, not full-auto.
C-03 · Priority: Immediate
Client Intelligence Profile
A lightweight, auto-populated client record triggered by first contact. Phone number = unique key. No manual entry required. Captures history, flags, and notes without ever opening a form.
P2 P6 P9 #crm #auto-capture
Minimum V0 fields: number, first contact date, source channel, risk flag, free-text note.
C-04 · Priority: Short-term
Inbound Message Triage
Automatic classification before the TDS has to read anything. Flags blacklisted numbers, harassment patterns, and first-contact from unknowns. Three control modes: manual, semi-auto, auto.
P3 P4 P13 #automation #filter
the co-founder: different users will want different levels of control — this must be configurable.
C-05 · Priority: Short-term
Pre-Booking Verification Flow
A structured check at the start of each new client interaction. Blacklist lookup + optional identity signal. Results in a green/amber/red before the TDS invests time in negotiation.
P6 P9 P10 #trust #kyc
Verification expectations vary by user. System surfaces intelligence — it doesn't prescribe protocol.
C-06 · Priority: V2
Availability Broadcasting
Declare availability in natural language. It propagates. No calendar UI. No grid. No slots to fill in manually on each platform. A simple on/off + shareable link covers 80% of the need.
P11 P4 #scheduling #no-interface
Full booking optimization (graph theory) is out of scope. V0: declare → share → confirm.
C-07 · Priority: V2
Revenue & Income Tracker
A lightweight session log for earnings — critical in a cash-dominant business. Inspired by The Dancer Resource (US, $6/mo). Natural fit for a second phase once operational trust is established.
P16 #financial #tracking
Mentioned once. Low priority for V0 but a natural V2 addition with high perceived value.

08 · Next Steps

What Must Happen
Before a Single Line of Code

User Research

01
3 Direct Mom-Test Interviews
Without the co-founder present. Past-behaviour questions only. "Tell me about the last time you received a message that made you uncomfortable. What did you do, step by step?"
02
Tool Stack Inventory (all 10 beta users)
Which messaging apps, phone OS, scheduling method, client notes system, platform subscriptions (BMG, F-Girl). Maps the real current state before designing the future state.
03
Blacklist Smoke Test
A shared private Google Sheet. 5 beta users. 2 weeks. Measure: how many numbers added? Do they check it proactively? This validates A1 before building anything.

Clarifications Needed

Q1
What exactly is BMG?
Platform name, scope, ownership. the co-founder knows a BMG contact — potential partner or competitor to profile carefully.
Q2
What is Aspasie's formal relationship with Scarlot?
NGO trust transfer + potential distribution channel. Already mentioned as an active demand signal.
Q3
Can the advisor interview the beta user directly?
The bizdev-oriented beta user is the richest anecdotal source in all 6 sessions. Primary interview candidate. She used Monday.com and called it impossible. That's gold-tier data.
Q4
What happened to the Israeli Telegram bot?
Closest existing model to the conversational-native vision. Shutdown reason matters for risk assessment.
The POC Proposal
the advisor's suggested next move: build a limited proof-of-concept independently, using AI-assisted coding tools, to test functional direction and user interaction without committing to full architecture.
01
WhatsApp or Telegram bot: receive a phone number, return blacklist status from a manually maintained list
02
Voice note or text → client record creation flow. No UI. No form. Natural language only.
03
Personal blacklist only in V0. No shared data. Avoids legal and trust complexity at this stage.
SUCCESS CRITERIA
At least 3 beta users try it organically — not just because the co-founder asked
At least 1 user contributes a blacklist entry unprompted
At least 1 user checks the blacklist before responding to a new contact
